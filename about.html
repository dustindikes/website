About

Hello! My name is Dustin Dikes.

I have been working as a full-stack web developer/software engineer for 15 years.

The first half of my career I built websites and administration systems for advertising. I also lead a team of 4-5 developers during that time, mentoring, managing the team's workload, and meeting with clients about new projects.

The second half of my career I have been working as a senior software engineer building enterprise web-based software, primarily in Typescript (React and Angular), C#, and SQL.

As of February 2025, I am working toward a Bachelor's in Cybersecurity. My goal is to jump into the cybersecurity industry and become a penetration tester. I would also like to start bug bounty hunting.
